Terms & Conditions and Refund Policy

Effective Date: 2026

1. About Us

TouchMe+ is a Sri Lankan sole proprietorship providing island-wide private bus booking services. We operate as a technology platform connecting customers with private bus operators for intercity travel, starting from the Eastern Province.

2. Booking & User Information

  • You must provide accurate personal details during booking.
  • Passengers under 15 must include a parent or guarantor NIC number.
  • A valid ID and ticket must be presented at boarding.
  • Bookings with false or incomplete information may be cancelled.

3. Payments

  • All payments must be made online via PayHere gateway.
  • Cash payments are not accepted.
  • All charges are in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR).
  • Payments are final except under Section 5 cancellation terms.

4. Ticket Confirmation

  • E-tickets are sent via email.
  • Ticket number is sent via SMS.
  • Tickets are valid only for booked date, time and route.
  • Buses will not wait for late arrivals.

5. Cancellation & Refund Policy

More Than 24 Hours Before Departure

  • 100% refund OR free reschedule (subject to availability).

Between 1–24 Hours Before Departure

  • 80% refund of ticket fare.

Less Than 1 Hour Before Departure

  • 50% refund of ticket fare.

No-Show Without Cancellation

  • No refund.
  • Exception: 50% refund with valid medical proof.

Important Notes:

  • Service fees are non-refundable.
  • Refunds processed within 7 working days.
  • Rescheduling subject to availability.

6. Child & Minor Policy

  • Children under 15 must be accompanied by an adult.
  • Infants under 1 may travel on an adult’s lap unless a seat is purchased.

7. Data Protection & Privacy

  • Personal information is collected only for ticketing and support.
  • Data is stored securely and not shared unlawfully.
  • We follow Sri Lankan Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).
  • You may request access, update, or deletion of your data.

8. Our Role

TouchMe+ is a booking platform, not the bus operator. We are not directly responsible for operator actions but will investigate reported issues.

9. Limitation of Liability

  • We are not liable for delays, accidents or losses caused by operators.
  • Maximum liability is limited to the ticket fare paid.

10. Disputes & Legal Process

Disputes will first be resolved through arbitration under Sri Lankan law. If unresolved, legal action may proceed in Sri Lankan courts.

11. Changes to Terms

We may update these Terms periodically. Updates become effective upon publication.
